Vegetable Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Buns
  • Cheese slice
  • Aloo tikki
  • Paneer
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Pasta sauce
  • Burger Topping Sauce
  • Oregano
  • Chilly Flakes

Instructions

  1. Prepare your crispy aaloo tikki
  2. Heat your pan and toast your buns a little
  3. Spread a tbsp topping sauce and pasta sauce
  4. Add aaloo tiiki, tomato slice, onion slice and a cube of paneer
  5. Add cheese slice
  6. On other bu, spread ketchup
  7. Sprinkle oregano and chilly flakes.
  8. Put your second bun on top, add sesame seeds and bake in oven.

Your delicious burger is soooooo readyy!
